Our Story

Care Rooted in Community

Founded with Purpose

Manzanita Health Care was founded in 2010 by a team of registered nurses and family caregivers who saw firsthand the lack of compassionate, professional home care for Bay Area seniors. Our name — Manzanita — is a native California plant known for its resilience and deep roots, values that define our care philosophy.

Our Mission

We believe every individual deserves to age with dignity, independence, and joy — in the place they call home. Our mission is to deliver the highest quality home health care through skilled, caring professionals who treat every patient like family.
